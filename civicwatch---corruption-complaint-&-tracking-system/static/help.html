How can we help?

Is my report really anonymous?

Yes. When you choose “File Anonymously”, we scrub all identifying metadata and avoid linking the report to your identity.

How long does an investigation take?

Initial reviews are typically done within 48 hours. Full investigations can take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity.

Can I edit my complaint?

Once submitted, you cannot edit the complaint. You can submit additional evidence referencing your tracking ID.

What is the legal protection?

The Whistleblower Protection Act of 2024 protects citizens filing genuine reports from retaliation.
